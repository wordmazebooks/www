WORD MAZE BOOKS

WORD PUZZLE BOOKS FOR YOURSELF, OR FOR A GIFT

Word Maze puzzles, cryptograms, and more!

I make word puzzle books of different sorts. Word Maze puzzles are a challenging type of word search where the words form a maze. Cryptograms are coded messages you have to decipher to read.

The puzzle books might be just what you are looking for.

  • Want to keep your mind sharp?
  • Want to keep your hands busy?
  • Want to improve concentration?
  • Want something to help relax?
  • Looking for a fun pastime?
  • Looking for puzzles with meaningful content?
  • Looking for a great gift?

Sample Word Maze Puzzle:
Find "SMILES ARE CONTAGIOUS"


Cryptograms: You need to solve a secret message by swapping out letters. For example, the secret message "KNFORK ZJR BGMLZDFGPK" can be "SMILES ARE CONTAGIOUS." Every K is an S, every N is an M, every F is an I, every O is an L, every R is an E, etc.

Find-a-Path: This is the traditional Word Maze puzzle. You are given an expression or a sentence and you have to find the whole thing in a grid of letters. This appeals most to people who like ♦️word search puzzles.

Category: These puzzles are harder than Find-a-Path. You are given a category, but not the actual words. Not only do you have to figure out the words, but you also have to find their path in the grid. This appeals most to people who like ♦️crosswords,♦️ anagrams, and ♦️cryptograms.
